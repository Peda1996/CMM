float overflow

What happened?

You tried to fill a float with a number which is to high or to low for this type.

Troubleshooting

An overflow can occour while performing a calculation which is result in a number which is outside the allowed range.
For example, that can happen if you multiply 2. big numbers.

Additional information

A floatingpoint number has a value range of ±1,4-45 to ±3,4+38.
